## Issues found and fixed

### F1. Missing `Exclude-A-Traj-Extreme-firstRV-RV-Propagated` in `exclude.levels` (BUG)

- Not triggered by test data (no test case produces this path), but a real latent bug
- **Fixed:** Added to `exclude.levels.adult` in `child_clean.R`
